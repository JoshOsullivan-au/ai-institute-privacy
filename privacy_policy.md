# Privacy Policy for AI Institute Vector Store Integration

**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Institute ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Custom GPT integration with vector store services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Query Data
- **Search Queries**: When you interact with our Custom GPT, we collect the search queries you submit
- **Usage Patterns**: We may collect information about how you use the service, including frequency and types of queries
- **Response Data**: We collect the responses generated by our AI system

### 2.2 Technical Information
- **API Usage**: Information about API calls made to our vector store integration
- **System Logs**: Technical logs for debugging and service improvement
- **Performance Metrics**: Response times and system performance data

### 2.3 Vector Store Data
- **Knowledge Base Content**: Our vector store contains AI Institute training materials, case studies, and educational content
- **Document Metadata**: Information about documents in our knowledge base including titles, sources, and timestamps

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Service Provision

### 3.2 Analytics and Improvement
- **Usage Analytics**: To understand how our service is used and identify areas for improvement
- **Performance Monitoring**: To ensure optimal service performance and reliability
- **Content Optimization**: To improve the relevance and quality of our knowledge base
